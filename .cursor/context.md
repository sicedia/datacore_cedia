# CEDIA context

CEDIA is a national Ecuadorian organization focused on **research and academic connectivity**. It brings together higher education institutions and research centers.

## Model focus

- **Contracts**: agreements with institutions or providers.
- **Products and services**: connectivity offerings, platforms, and support.
- The **core model** is independent of sources; it integrates concepts from multiple systems without replicating their structure.

This context should be assumed in all assistant responses.
